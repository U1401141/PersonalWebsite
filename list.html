What is Goshuin-cho?
A Goshuin-cho is a special book used to collect handwritten seals (Goshuin) from Shinto shrines and Buddhist temples in Japan. Each seal is a unique piece of calligraphy serving as a record of one's visit.
*Note: I collect these purely for artistic appreciation and travel memories. There is absolutely no religious meaning or intent behind this collection.
